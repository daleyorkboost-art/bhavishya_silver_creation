Our Legacy

Crafting Memories,
Defining Luxury.

Founded in the heart of Faridabad in 2010, Manish Jewellers 21D was born from a singular vision: to bring uncompromising purity and world-class artistry to discerning clientele. What began as a modest family-run atelier has flourished into one of North India's most respected jewellery destinations.

We believe that true luxury is personal. It is the weight of an MMTC PAMP certified gold bar, the brilliance of a flawless diamond, and the intricate filigree of our exclusive Bhavishya Silver Creation line. Our sub-brand is a tribute to the future — where contemporary aesthetics meet ancestral craftsmanship.

Over the last decade and a half, we have cultivated partnerships with India's most prestigious houses—including GIVA, ACPL True Silver, EMRALD, KUNDAN, and AUGMONT—ensuring that our vaults hold only the exceptional. From bespoke bridal trousseaus to contemporary daily wear, your legacy begins here.
